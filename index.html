Candice Kiser is a clarinetist with the Indianapolis Chamber Orchestra and the Richmond Symphony Orchestra. She has served as a guest musician in the Indianapolis Symphony Orchestra, the Erie Philharmonic, and the Akron Symphony.

Previously, Ms. Kiser served on the faculty of Slippery Rock University of Pennsylvania and Grove City College. She earned the Bachelor of Music degree from East Carolina University and the Master of Music degree from the Indiana University Jacobs School of Music.

She has been hailed by critics for her "ravishing dark straight tone, beautiful legato, and dead-on intonation....gorgeous."(Roanoke Times)
